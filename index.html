Epic battle story about saving the Earth!

You were sent out to space to save the day! A nasty mysterious triangle-shaped UFO came to take out our planet... It looks to be invincible, but NASA found out it is actually vulnerable to nuclear radiation, that's why they implemented a radiation emitting device in your cool big red spaceship!

The closer you are to the UFO - the more powerful the radiation cloud gets! Just be careful - triangle tends to go imba every now and then - if I were you (a badass spaceship captain) I'd certainly look for a safe spot as soon as possible!

Use W,A,S,D or the arrows to control your spaceship!

Good luck!
